### todoer

Search your codebase for `TODO` comments and get a summary of the amount and their age.

### Usage

`./todoer --r <reponame> --strict`

### Sample output

```
  56 - 0.6 years
  14 - 1.2 years
  13 - 1.7 years
  11 - 1.6 years
  11 - 0.4 years
   8 - 2.0 years
   5 - 2.4 years
   5 - 2.2 years
   5 - 1.1 years
   4 - 3.2 years
   4 - 2.3 years
```

### Dependencies

You will need both [ack](http://beyondgrep.com/) and [git](http://git-scm.com/).
